About Us

World's biggest community for content creators

Our networking website will connect content creators with organisations to create mutual beneficial opportunities. Instead of simply being just a stay connected website, it will help content creators find work and the organisations can find ideal advertisers for brand recognition and to promote brand loyalty.
Basic functionality
• Users can create their profiles and can add their social media link to show their reach and impression. They can also approach various companies by sending them promotional request.
• The organisations looking for advertising their product or services can review their profiles and rankings. Once they have chosen the influencer(s) of their choice, they can send them a promotion request.
• The promotional request can also be sorted according to the one’s relevance (type of audience they are engaged with), their engagement, price etc.
Utility
Our platform will provide special support to small entrepreneurs to advertise their product and help them reach out to the local public.
